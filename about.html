All About Us!

You Pet'cha was founded by a pet lover for pet lovers! We want to ensure that with everything you have on your plate, you can trust knowing that when you need us to, we'll fill you pets' plate. We are based in the area of Trinity, FL, USA. We can do pet-sitting in homes within 15 miles, or we can board your pet here so long as you transport them to us. Kat Smith is our proud business owner, and she takes the care of animals seriously. Kat has had pets since the age of 7 and she has always taken the time to properly research and care for them, even at a young age. She has owned, and is therefore well versed in the care of dogs, cats, birds, hamsters, and fish. Kat takes pride in her small animal knowledge, as not many services offer knowledgable care on them. We offer a free meet and greet with us so we can get to know you and your animal companion while we work out the plan that's best for you!
